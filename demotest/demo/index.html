Demo Mode

Demo Mode

Check the integration of products in the online shop with a demo mode. There is no communication with the financial service provider but only between the online shop and QENTA. A connection to the Internet is required to gain access to QPAY Checkout Page and the payment process is only simulated displaying the financial service provider pages as images and the possibility to select a successful or failed payment process result. The executed payments are not displayed in the QPC Payment Center.
