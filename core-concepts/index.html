Core Concepts

Resources

The QENTA Banking as a Service Platform API (here) provides resources to handle all the necessary processes and flows you might encounter with your program.

These resources are the most important ones to understand and are required to create card programs:

  • A Legal Entity resource represents the identity of the beneficial owner of the account with QENTA BaaS.

  • An Account Holder resource represents the cardholder. Each Account Holder must be associated with a Legal Entity, which QENTA will KYC as part of the onboarding.

  • A Balance Account resource represents the financial synchronization point for the funds available to linked Card resources. Multiple Balance Account resources per Account Holder can be created.

  • A Card resource represents the users physical or virtual card. It spends the funds provided by the linked Balance Account. Multiple Card resources per Balance Account can be created. A card resource must have an associated residential address, which QENTA will verify as part of the card onboarding.

Synchronization

The QENTA Issuing Platform uses Event resources to handle asynchronous processing flows with you. This is especially important to hold the Balance Account in sync with cards based on accounts in your control. You will send us load/unload requests and we will send you account adjustment events.

The Webhook resource provides the configuration for sending the Event resources.
