Getting Started

Let’s get going! Here’s how to start building quickly with us

Getting Your Qenta Portal API Key

Sign up here with some basic information defining your program, and we’ll generate you access to the Qenta BaaS Portal.

From there, you’ll be able to create and manage API keys to begin testing out what our platform looks like.

You can also go ahead and schedule a 30-minute intro call here or send us an email with any questions about how our embedded finance offering fits into your product at baas_sales@qenta.com .

Defining your card program

We will need the following data to create your payment program:

  • Basic information about you and your customers (Which services are you currently providing? Who do you serve - small business owners in France? Diaspora communities in Austria?)

  • The card types you want to issue. (Debit / Credit / Prepaid ; Consumer / Corporate)

  • The account structures you will need

Your Qenta contact will help you to define the card program based on your use cases.

A new card program involves setting the following:

The account structure and synchronization

A new program must have a defined bank account structure and to be represented in the Qenta Banking as a Service Platform.

Our platform default is a simple setup assigning one account holder to one balance account with one card (= 1:1:1), but we can also provide enhanced setups with multiple account holders, balance accounts and cards (n:n:n) based on your requirements.

If a custom setup is required, we will work with your team to identify any gaps in your setup which might be needed to reserve and settle funds.

The final result will be the account mapping and flow of funds.

The Card Type

Once the account structure is clear, we then define the card type which you will issue.

Card types are shaped by key program feature decisions such as issuing physical or virtual cards, the country of issuance, and whether the card will be debit, credit or prepaid model.

Physical cards are produced and shipped to the cardholder. They can be used for e-commerce, payment terminals and ATMs.

Virtual cards are digital products with no physical form. They will be shown in your app to the cardholder and can be used for e-commerce only. We can also enable you to convert virtual cards to physical ones.

For details, please continue with Card Management section.

Your Qenta contact will help you with the visual design and parameters of the card. As we work with key partners such as VISA and our partner banking network, we will work side by side with you to ensure that your card design and related marketing meets key regulatory and marketing requirements.

Accessing your Qenta Issuing Platform Test Setup

We will provide you a test program matching your defined card program. You will get access to :

  • The Qenta Issuing Platform API (link to the swagger asciidoc)

    • Manage your resources

    • Simulate transactions

  • The Qenta Portal (linkt to the qenta portal test instance)

    • Find, see and manage your resources

Integrate with us

Your Qenta contact and implementation specialist will help you to make sure technical integration happens smoothly.

Go live

After we successfully test your integration to the platform, it’s time to go live!
